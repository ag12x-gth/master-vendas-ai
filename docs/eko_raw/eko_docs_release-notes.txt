Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about Fellou
How is an AI browser different from a chat assistant?
An AI chat assistant is reactive, only answering your questions. An AI Browser with agentic AI can automatically run redundant work for you and even proactively help you to complete tasks you've forgotten.
The biggest differences:
Deep action:
Fellou independently plan and execute complex web and desktop based tasks across multiple apps to achieve your goals.
Deep search:
Fellou automates in-depth research across the entire internet, including logged-in accounts on platforms like X, Reddit or Salefore.
Dynamic Multitasking:
Fellou runs multiple tasks simultaneously in its back-end workspace, while users browse other sites.
Agentic memory:
Fellou can offer proactive, personalized help by connecting browser history, chat history, contextual page data, and personal knowledge base.
Can AI browsers do more than just summarize pages and organize tabs?
Yes, it goes far beyond basic summarization. An Agentic AI Browser automates complex work across the web.
Conducts in-depth research to generate reports.
Automates data entry and social media management.
Automatically executes custom, multi-step workflows you define to achieve specific goals.
Can AI automate my work across different apps and websites?
Yes, cross-platform automation is a core function of an Agentic Browser.
It executes complex workflows across different apps and websites.
It can pull data from one platform (like LinkedIn) and use it in another (like Notion).
This provides a more integrated automation experience than API-based tools.
Does it work on sites that require a login or CAPTCHA?
Yes, a key capability of our AI Browser is navigating the real web.
It is designed to handle logins, authentication, and even CAPTCHA challenges.
Advanced AI can solve CAPTCHAs with high accuracy by simulating human behavior.
This enables automation on secure sites.
Can I see and control exactly what the AI agent is doing?
Yes, our AI Browser is designed for complete transparency and user control.
Visually inspect and edit the AI Browser's automatic workflow before it runs.
Monitor the agent's progress in real-time.
Intervene at any step to adjust or halt the task, ensuring you always have full oversight.
What can AI Browser create other than answering questions by text?
Yes, it's a multi-modal creation tool. Our AI Browser can generate:
In-depth research reports that include text, images, and charts.
Creative content such as images, logos, SVGs, and even music.
Structured documents like presentations (PPTs) and spreadsheets (Excel).
Code for web pages and applications‚Äîfrom websites and games to interactive models‚Äîusing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
Can I schedule tasks to run automatically in the future?
Yes, you can schedule any complex workflow to run automatically.
Go to Fellou home and find tasks. You will see scheduled settings
Click +New Schedule to set tasks to execute at a specific time or on a recurring basis.
This goes beyond simple reminders, enabling full delegation of routine digital work. It's ideal for recurring reports or data monitoring.
Can I build my own custom AI agents and workflows?
Yes, Fellou is an extensible platform for developers.
Use our open-source Eko 2.0 framework to build custom AI agents.
Create powerful, intervenable agents that run within the Fellou AI Browser by using a hybrid of natural language and JavaScript.
Tailor automation to your specific needs.
